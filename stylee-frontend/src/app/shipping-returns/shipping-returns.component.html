Shipping & Returns

1. Shipping Information

We are committed to delivering your orders as quickly and efficiently as possible. Our shipping options are designed to cater to your needs.

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2-3 business days.
  • Free Shipping: Available on orders above $50.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number to monitor its progress. Please ensure your shipping address is accurate to avoid any delays.

2. Returns Policy

We want you to love your purchase! If you are not completely satisfied, we offer a hassle-free return policy.

  • Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be in their original condition and packaging.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping fees for returns are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective or damaged.

To initiate a return, please contact us with your order details, and our support team will assist you.

3. Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective, please notify us within 7 days of receipt. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost.

4. International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Delivery times and shipping costs vary based on location. Customs fees, if applicable, are the customer's responsibility.

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact us.
